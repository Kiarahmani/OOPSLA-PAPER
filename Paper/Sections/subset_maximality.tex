For all operation executions of the form: 
\begin{smathpar}
(\E,op_{<s,i>}) 
    \;\xrightarrow{V}\;
  (\E',\eff) 
,\end{smathpar}

execution states $E''$ such that:
\begin{smathpar}
(\E,op_{<s,i>})\;\xrightarrow{V}\;(\E'',\eff)
\end{smathpar}
then    
$\small (\E''.vis^{-1}(\eta)) \subseteq
(\E'.vis^{-1}(\eta))$
